FAQ - Rote Games

'Rote Games' is an app that aims to help enhance your memory through games where you must remember and repeat sequences of elements.

The app has 4 different game modes:

  1. Classic mode: the user has to repeat the elements in the same order in which they appear.
  2. Reverse mode: the user has to repeat the elements in reverse order i.e. from the last to the first element of the sequence.
  3. Random mode: each round, the game generates a different sequence. The user has to repeat it in the same order in which the elements appear.
  4. Mixed mode: the user has to repeat the sequence in the order specified at the beginning. Every 5 rounds, a new sequence is generated and the order to enter the elements may change.

The subscription grants access to two additional game modes:

  1. Random mode
  2. Mixed mode

Also, ads are removed.

There are three subscription plans available:

  1. Montly plan: grants access for 1 month.
  2. Semi-Annual plan: grants access for 6 months.
  3. Annual plan: grants access for 1 year.

All plans grant the same level of access, they only differ in the subscription term.

If it is your first time subscribing, you will receive a free trial period of 7 days (if you chose the monthly plan), 14 days (if you chose the semi-annual plan) or 30 days (if you chose the annual plan). The subscription will be charged and will begin once the free trial period ends.[1]

The subscription will automatically renew and you will receive the charge with the subscription amount at the end of each period, unless you cancel the subscription at least 24 hours before the renewal date. To manage your subscription, access your Apple ID profile in your device settings and look for the Subscriptions section.

You can cancel your subscription anytime.

[1] The free trial period does not apply when renewing a subscription or if you were a subscriber previously.
